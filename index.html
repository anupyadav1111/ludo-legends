Ludo Rules

Objective

Be the first player to move all 4 tokens from home to the center finish area.

How to Play

  • Each player rolls the dice on their turn
  • Roll a 6 to bring a token out of home
  • Rolling a 6 gives you another turn
  • Move tokens clockwise around the board
  • Landing on opponent's token sends it back home
  • Safe spots (marked with stars) protect tokens
  • Enter home column when you complete the circuit
  • Exact roll needed to reach center finish

Winning

First player to get all 4 tokens to the center wins!
